Deep Forest Green

#182D09

Deep Forest Green belongs to the color family Dark Spring Green. It is of a low brightness and a high saturation.
Deep Forest Green corresponds to the hex code #182D09.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 9% red, 18% green and 4% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 95° (out of 360°), 80% saturation and 18% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 47% cyan, 0% magenta, 80% yellow and 82% black pigments.
