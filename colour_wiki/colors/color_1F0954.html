Dark Indigo

#1F0954

Dark Indigo belongs to the color family Dark Purple. It is of a medium brightness and a high saturation.
Dark Indigo corresponds to the hex code #1F0954.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 63% cyan, 89% magenta, 0% yellow and 67% black pigments.
