Stark White

#E5D7BD

Stark White corresponds to the hex code #E5D7BD.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 90% red, 84% green and 74% blue components.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 6% magenta, 17% yellow and 10% black pigments.
