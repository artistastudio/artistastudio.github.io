Spearmint

#1EF876

Spearmint belongs to the color family Ocean Green. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Spearmint corresponds to the hex code #1EF876.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 88% cyan, 0% magenta, 52% yellow and 3% black pigments.
