Ultramarine Blue

#4166F5

Ultramarine Blue belongs to the color family Blue. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Ultramarine Blue corresponds to the hex code #4166F5.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 73% cyan, 58% magenta, 0% yellow and 4% black pigments.
