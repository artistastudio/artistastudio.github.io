Ecru White

#F5F3E5

Ecru White belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Ecru White corresponds to the hex code #F5F3E5.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 96% red, 95% green and 90% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 52° (out of 360°), 7% saturation and 96% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 1% magenta, 7% yellow and 4% black pigments.
