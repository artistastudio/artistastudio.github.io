Coral Blue

#AFDCEC

Coral Blue belongs to the color family Pastel Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Coral Blue corresponds to the hex code #AFDCEC.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 26% cyan, 7% magenta, 0% yellow and 7% black pigments.
