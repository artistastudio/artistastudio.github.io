Persian Pink

#F77FBE

Persian Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Persian Pink corresponds to the hex code #F77FBE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 97% red, 50% green and 75% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 328° (out of 360°), 49% saturation and 97% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 49% magenta, 23% yellow and 3% black pigments.
