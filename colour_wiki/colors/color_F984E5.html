Pale Magenta

#F984E5

Pale Magenta belongs to the color family Pastel Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Pale Magenta corresponds to the hex code #F984E5.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 98% red, 52% green and 90% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 309° (out of 360°), 47% saturation and 98% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 47% magenta, 8% yellow and 2% black pigments.
