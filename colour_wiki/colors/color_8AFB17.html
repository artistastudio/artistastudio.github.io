Chartreuse

#8AFB17

Chartreuse belongs to the color family Spring Green. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Chartreuse corresponds to the hex code #8AFB17.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 54% red, 98% green and 9% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 89° (out of 360°), 91% saturation and 98% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 45% cyan, 0% magenta, 91% yellow and 2% black pigments.
