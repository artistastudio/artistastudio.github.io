Dawn Pink

#F3E9E5

Dawn Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Orange. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Dawn Pink corresponds to the hex code #F3E9E5.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 95% red, 91% green and 90% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 17° (out of 360°), 6% saturation and 95% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 4% magenta, 6% yellow and 5% black pigments.
