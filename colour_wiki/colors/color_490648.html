Deep Violet

#490648

Deep Violet belongs to the color family Dark Magenta. It is of a low brightness and a high saturation.
Deep Violet corresponds to the hex code #490648.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 29% red, 2% green and 28% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 300° (out of 360°), 92% saturation and 29% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 92% magenta, 1% yellow and 71% black pigments.
