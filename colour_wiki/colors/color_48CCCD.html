Medium Turquoise

#48CCCD

Medium Turquoise belongs to the color family Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Medium Turquoise corresponds to the hex code #48CCCD.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 28% red, 80% green and 80% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 180° (out of 360°), 65% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 65% cyan, 0% magenta, 0% yellow and 20% black pigments.
