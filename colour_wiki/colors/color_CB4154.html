Brick Red

#CB4154

Brick Red belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Brick Red corresponds to the hex code #CB4154.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 25% green and 33% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 351° (out of 360°), 68% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 68% magenta, 59% yellow and 20% black pigments.
