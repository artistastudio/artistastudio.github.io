Sepia Black

#2B0202

Sepia Black belongs to the color family Dark Red. It is of a low brightness and a high saturation.
Sepia Black corresponds to the hex code #2B0202.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 17% red, 1% green and 1% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 0° (out of 360°), 95% saturation and 17% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 95% magenta, 95% yellow and 83% black pigments.
