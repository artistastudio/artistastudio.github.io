Pale Gold

#E6BE8A

Pale Gold belongs to the color family Pastel Orange. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Pale Gold corresponds to the hex code #E6BE8A.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 90% red, 75% green and 54% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 34° (out of 360°), 40% saturation and 90% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 17% magenta, 40% yellow and 10% black pigments.
