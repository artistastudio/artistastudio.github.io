Venetian Red

#C80815

Venetian Red belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Venetian Red corresponds to the hex code #C80815.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 78% red, 3% green and 8% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 355° (out of 360°), 96% saturation and 78% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 96% magenta, 89% yellow and 22% black pigments.
