Light Carmine Pink

#E66771

Light Carmine Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Light Carmine Pink corresponds to the hex code #E66771.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 90% red, 40% green and 44% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 354° (out of 360°), 55% saturation and 90% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 55% magenta, 51% yellow and 10% black pigments.
