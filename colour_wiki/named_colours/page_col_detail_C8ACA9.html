Pinkish Grey

#C8ACA9

Pinkish Grey belongs to the color family Pastel Red. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Pinkish Grey corresponds to the hex code #C8ACA9.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 78% red, 67% green and 66% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 6° (out of 360°), 16% saturation and 78% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 14% magenta, 16% yellow and 22% black pigments.
