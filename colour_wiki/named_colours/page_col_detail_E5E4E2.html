Platinum

#E5E4E2

Platinum belongs to the color family Pastel Orange. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Platinum corresponds to the hex code #E5E4E2.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 90% red, 89% green and 89% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 40° (out of 360°), 1% saturation and 90% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 0% magenta, 1% yellow and 10% black pigments.
