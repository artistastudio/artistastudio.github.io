Bright Magenta

#FF08E8

Bright Magenta belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Bright Magenta corresponds to the hex code #FF08E8.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 100% red, 3% green and 91% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 305° (out of 360°), 97% saturation and 100% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 97% magenta, 9% yellow and 0% black pigments.
