Tiffany Blue

#0ABAB5

Tiffany Blue belongs to the color family Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Tiffany Blue corresponds to the hex code #0ABAB5.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 4% red, 73% green and 71% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 178° (out of 360°), 95% saturation and 73% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 95% cyan, 0% magenta, 3% yellow and 27% black pigments.
