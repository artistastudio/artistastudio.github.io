Amethyst

#9B5FC0

Amethyst belongs to the color family Purple. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Amethyst corresponds to the hex code #9B5FC0.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 61% red, 37% green and 75% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 276° (out of 360°), 51% saturation and 75% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 19% cyan, 51% magenta, 0% yellow and 25% black pigments.
