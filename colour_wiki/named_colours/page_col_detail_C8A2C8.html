Lilac

#C8A2C8

Lilac belongs to the color family Pastel Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Lilac corresponds to the hex code #C8A2C8.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 78% red, 64% green and 78% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 299° (out of 360°), 19% saturation and 78% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 19% magenta, 0% yellow and 22% black pigments.
