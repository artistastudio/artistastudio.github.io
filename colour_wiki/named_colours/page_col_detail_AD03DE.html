Vibrant Purple

#AD03DE

Vibrant Purple belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Vibrant Purple corresponds to the hex code #AD03DE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 68% red, 1% green and 87% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 286° (out of 360°), 99% saturation and 87% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 22% cyan, 99% magenta, 0% yellow and 13% black pigments.
