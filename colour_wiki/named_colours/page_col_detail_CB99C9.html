Pastel Violet

#CB99C9

Pastel Violet belongs to the color family Pastel Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Pastel Violet corresponds to the hex code #CB99C9.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 60% green and 79% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 302° (out of 360°), 25% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 25% magenta, 1% yellow and 20% black pigments.
