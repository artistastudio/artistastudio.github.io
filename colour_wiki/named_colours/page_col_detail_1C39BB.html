Persian Blue

#1C39BB

Persian Blue belongs to the color family Blue. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Persian Blue corresponds to the hex code #1C39BB.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 11% red, 22% green and 73% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 228° (out of 360°), 85% saturation and 73% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 85% cyan, 70% magenta, 0% yellow and 27% black pigments.
