Greyish Green

#82A67D

Greyish Green belongs to the color family Pastel Green. It is of a medium brightness and a low saturation.
Greyish Green corresponds to the hex code #82A67D.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 22% cyan, 0% magenta, 25% yellow and 35% black pigments.
