Havelock Blue

#5590D9

Havelock Blue belongs to the color family Sky Blue. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Havelock Blue corresponds to the hex code #5590D9.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 61% cyan, 34% magenta, 0% yellow and 15% black pigments.
