Rusty Orange

#CD5909

Rusty Orange belongs to the color family Orange. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Rusty Orange corresponds to the hex code #CD5909.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 35% green and 4% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 24° (out of 360°), 96% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 57% magenta, 96% yellow and 20% black pigments.
