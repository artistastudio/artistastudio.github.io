Olive Yellow

#C2B709

Olive Yellow belongs to the color family Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Olive Yellow corresponds to the hex code #C2B709.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 72% green and 4% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 56° (out of 360°), 95% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 6% magenta, 95% yellow and 24% black pigments.
