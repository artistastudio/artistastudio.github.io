Aubergine

#3D0734

Aubergine belongs to the color family Dark Magenta. It is of a low brightness and a high saturation.
Aubergine corresponds to the hex code #3D0734.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 24% red, 3% green and 20% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 309° (out of 360°), 89% saturation and 24% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 89% magenta, 15% yellow and 76% black pigments.
