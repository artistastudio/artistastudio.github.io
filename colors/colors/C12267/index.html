Burnt Pink

#C12267

Burnt Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Burnt Pink corresponds to the hex code #C12267.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 13% green and 40% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 333° (out of 360°), 82% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 82% magenta, 47% yellow and 24% black pigments.
