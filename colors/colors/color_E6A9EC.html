Blush Pink

#E6A9EC

Blush Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Blush Pink corresponds to the hex code #E6A9EC.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 3% cyan, 28% magenta, 0% yellow and 7% black pigments.
