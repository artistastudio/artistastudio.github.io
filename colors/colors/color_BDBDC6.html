French Grey

#BDBDC6

French Grey belongs to the color family Pastel Blue. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
French Grey corresponds to the hex code #BDBDC6.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 74% red, 74% green and 78% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 239° (out of 360°), 5% saturation and 78% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 5% cyan, 5% magenta, 0% yellow and 22% black pigments.
