Carmine Pink

#EB4C42

Carmine Pink belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Carmine Pink corresponds to the hex code #EB4C42.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 92% red, 30% green and 26% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 4° (out of 360°), 72% saturation and 92% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 68% magenta, 72% yellow and 8% black pigments.
