Rose Pink

#FF66CC

Rose Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Rose Pink corresponds to the hex code #FF66CC.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 100% red, 40% green and 80% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 319° (out of 360°), 60% saturation and 100% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 60% magenta, 20% yellow and 0% black pigments.
