Pale Red

#D9544D

Pale Red belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Pale Red corresponds to the hex code #D9544D.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 85% red, 33% green and 30% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 3° (out of 360°), 65% saturation and 85% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 61% magenta, 65% yellow and 15% black pigments.
