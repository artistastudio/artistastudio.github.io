Purple Pink

#E03FD8

Purple Pink belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Purple Pink corresponds to the hex code #E03FD8.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 88% red, 25% green and 85% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 302° (out of 360°), 72% saturation and 88% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 72% magenta, 4% yellow and 12% black pigments.
