Turquoise Green

#04F489

Turquoise Green belongs to the color family Ocean Green. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Turquoise Green corresponds to the hex code #04F489.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 98% cyan, 0% magenta, 44% yellow and 4% black pigments.
