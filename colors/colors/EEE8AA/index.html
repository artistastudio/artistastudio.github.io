Pale Goldenrod

#EEE8AA

Pale Goldenrod belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Pale Goldenrod corresponds to the hex code #EEE8AA.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 93% red, 91% green and 67% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 55° (out of 360°), 29% saturation and 93% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 3% magenta, 29% yellow and 7% black pigments.
