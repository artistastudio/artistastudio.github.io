Dirty Yellow

#CDC50A

Dirty Yellow belongs to the color family Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Dirty Yellow corresponds to the hex code #CDC50A.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 77% green and 4% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 57° (out of 360°), 95% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 4% magenta, 95% yellow and 20% black pigments.
