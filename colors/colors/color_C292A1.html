Light Mauve

#C292A1

Light Mauve belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Light Mauve corresponds to the hex code #C292A1.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 57% green and 63% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 340° (out of 360°), 25% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 25% magenta, 17% yellow and 24% black pigments.
