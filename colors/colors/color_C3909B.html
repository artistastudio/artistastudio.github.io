Grey Pink

#C3909B

Grey Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Grey Pink corresponds to the hex code #C3909B.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 56% green and 61% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 346° (out of 360°), 26% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 26% magenta, 21% yellow and 24% black pigments.
