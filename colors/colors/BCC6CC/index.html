Metallic Silver

#BCC6CC

Metallic Silver belongs to the color family Pastel Sky Blue. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Metallic Silver corresponds to the hex code #BCC6CC.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 74% red, 78% green and 80% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 202° (out of 360°), 8% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 8% cyan, 3% magenta, 0% yellow and 20% black pigments.
