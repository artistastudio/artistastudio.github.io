Bluish Green

#10A674

Bluish Green belongs to the color family Ocean Green. It is of a medium brightness and a high saturation.
Bluish Green corresponds to the hex code #10A674.

In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 160° (out of 360°), 90% saturation and 65% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 90% cyan, 0% magenta, 30% yellow and 35% black pigments.
