Pale Violet

#CEAEFA

Pale Violet belongs to the color family Pastel Purple. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Pale Violet corresponds to the hex code #CEAEFA.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 81% red, 68% green and 98% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 265° (out of 360°), 30% saturation and 98% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 18% cyan, 30% magenta, 0% yellow and 2% black pigments.
