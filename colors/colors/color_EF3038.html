Deep Carmine Pink

#EF3038

Deep Carmine Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Deep Carmine Pink corresponds to the hex code #EF3038.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 80% magenta, 77% yellow and 6% black pigments.
