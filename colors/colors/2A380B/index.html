Turtle Green

#2A380B

Turtle Green belongs to the color family Dark Spring Green. It is of a low brightness and a high saturation.
Turtle Green corresponds to the hex code #2A380B.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 16% red, 22% green and 4% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 78° (out of 360°), 80% saturation and 22% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 25% cyan, 0% magenta, 80% yellow and 78% black pigments.
