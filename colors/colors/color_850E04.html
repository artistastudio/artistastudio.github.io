Indian Red

#850E04

Indian Red belongs to the color family Red. It is of a medium brightness and a high saturation.
Indian Red corresponds to the hex code #850E04.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 52% red, 5% green and 2% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 5° (out of 360°), 97% saturation and 52% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 89% magenta, 97% yellow and 48% black pigments.
