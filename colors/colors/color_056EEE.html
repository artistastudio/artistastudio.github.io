Cerulean Blue

#056EEE

Cerulean Blue belongs to the color family Sky Blue. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Cerulean Blue corresponds to the hex code #056EEE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 2% red, 43% green and 93% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 212° (out of 360°), 98% saturation and 93% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 98% cyan, 54% magenta, 0% yellow and 7% black pigments.
