Pastel Purple

#B39EB5

Pastel Purple belongs to the color family Pastel Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Pastel Purple corresponds to the hex code #B39EB5.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 70% red, 62% green and 71% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 294° (out of 360°), 13% saturation and 71% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 1% cyan, 13% magenta, 0% yellow and 29% black pigments.
