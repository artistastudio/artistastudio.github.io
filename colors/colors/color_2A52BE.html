Cerulean Blue

#2A52BE

Cerulean Blue belongs to the color family Sky Blue. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Cerulean Blue corresponds to the hex code #2A52BE.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 78% cyan, 57% magenta, 0% yellow and 25% black pigments.
