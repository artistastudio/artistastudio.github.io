Medium Jungle Green

#1C352D

Medium Jungle Green belongs to the color family Dark Pastel Ocean Green. It is of a low brightness and a medium saturation.
Medium Jungle Green corresponds to the hex code #1C352D.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 47% cyan, 0% magenta, 15% yellow and 79% black pigments.
