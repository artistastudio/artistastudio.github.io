Faded Green

#7BB274

Faded Green belongs to the color family Pastel Green. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Faded Green corresponds to the hex code #7BB274.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 48% red, 70% green and 45% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 113° (out of 360°), 35% saturation and 70% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 31% cyan, 0% magenta, 35% yellow and 30% black pigments.
