Beige

#F5F5DC

Beige belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Beige corresponds to the hex code #F5F5DC.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 96% red, 96% green and 86% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 60° (out of 360°), 10% saturation and 96% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 0% magenta, 10% yellow and 4% black pigments.
