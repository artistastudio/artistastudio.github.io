Persian Indigo

#32127A

Persian Indigo belongs to the color family Dark Purple. It is of a medium brightness and a high saturation.
Persian Indigo corresponds to the hex code #32127A.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 20% red, 7% green and 48% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 258° (out of 360°), 85% saturation and 48% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 59% cyan, 85% magenta, 0% yellow and 52% black pigments.
