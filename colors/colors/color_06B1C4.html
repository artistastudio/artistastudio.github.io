Turquoise Blue

#06B1C4

Turquoise Blue belongs to the color family Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Turquoise Blue corresponds to the hex code #06B1C4.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 2% red, 69% green and 77% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 185° (out of 360°), 97% saturation and 77% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 97% cyan, 10% magenta, 0% yellow and 23% black pigments.
