Kiwi Green

#8EE53F

Kiwi Green belongs to the color family Spring Green. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Kiwi Green corresponds to the hex code #8EE53F.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 56% red, 90% green and 25% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 91° (out of 360°), 72% saturation and 90% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 38% cyan, 0% magenta, 72% yellow and 10% black pigments.
