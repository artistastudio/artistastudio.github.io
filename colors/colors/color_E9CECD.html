Oyster Pink

#E9CECD

Oyster Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Red. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Oyster Pink corresponds to the hex code #E9CECD.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 91% red, 81% green and 80% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 2° (out of 360°), 12% saturation and 91% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 12% magenta, 12% yellow and 9% black pigments.
