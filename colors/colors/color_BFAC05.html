Muddy Yellow

#BFAC05

Muddy Yellow belongs to the color family Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Muddy Yellow corresponds to the hex code #BFAC05.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 75% red, 67% green and 2% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 54° (out of 360°), 97% saturation and 75% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 10% magenta, 97% yellow and 25% black pigments.
