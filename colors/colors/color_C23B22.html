Dark Pastel Red

#C23B22

Dark Pastel Red belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Dark Pastel Red corresponds to the hex code #C23B22.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 23% green and 13% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 9° (out of 360°), 82% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 70% magenta, 82% yellow and 24% black pigments.
