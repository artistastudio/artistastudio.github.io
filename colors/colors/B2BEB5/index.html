Ash Grey

#B2BEB5

Ash Grey belongs to the color family Pastel Green. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Ash Grey corresponds to the hex code #B2BEB5.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 70% red, 75% green and 71% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 135° (out of 360°), 6% saturation and 75% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 6% cyan, 0% magenta, 5% yellow and 25% black pigments.
