Slime Green

#BCE954

Slime Green belongs to the color family Spring Green. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Slime Green corresponds to the hex code #BCE954.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 19% cyan, 0% magenta, 64% yellow and 9% black pigments.
