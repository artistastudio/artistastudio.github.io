Jade Green

#2BAF6A

Jade Green belongs to the color family Ocean Green. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Jade Green corresponds to the hex code #2BAF6A.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 75% cyan, 0% magenta, 39% yellow and 31% black pigments.
