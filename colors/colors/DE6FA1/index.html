Thulian Pink

#DE6FA1

Thulian Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Thulian Pink corresponds to the hex code #DE6FA1.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 87% red, 44% green and 63% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 332° (out of 360°), 50% saturation and 87% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 50% magenta, 27% yellow and 13% black pigments.
