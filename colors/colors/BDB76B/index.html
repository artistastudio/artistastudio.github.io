Dark Khaki

#BDB76B

Dark Khaki belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Dark Khaki corresponds to the hex code #BDB76B.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 74% red, 72% green and 42% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 55° (out of 360°), 43% saturation and 74% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 3% magenta, 43% yellow and 26% black pigments.
