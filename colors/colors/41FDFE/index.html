Bright Cyan

#41FDFE

Bright Cyan belongs to the color family Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Bright Cyan corresponds to the hex code #41FDFE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 25% red, 99% green and 100% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 180° (out of 360°), 74% saturation and 100% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 74% cyan, 0% magenta, 0% yellow and 0% black pigments.
