Sea Pink

#ED989E

Sea Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Sea Pink corresponds to the hex code #ED989E.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 36% magenta, 33% yellow and 7% black pigments.
