Dusty Blue

#5A86AD

Dusty Blue belongs to the color family Pastel Sky Blue. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Dusty Blue corresponds to the hex code #5A86AD.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 35% red, 53% green and 68% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 208° (out of 360°), 48% saturation and 68% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 48% cyan, 23% magenta, 0% yellow and 32% black pigments.
