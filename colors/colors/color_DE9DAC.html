Faded Pink

#DE9DAC

Faded Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Faded Pink corresponds to the hex code #DE9DAC.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 87% red, 62% green and 67% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 345° (out of 360°), 29% saturation and 87% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 29% magenta, 23% yellow and 13% black pigments.
