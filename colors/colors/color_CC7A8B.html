Dusky Pink

#CC7A8B

Dusky Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Dusky Pink corresponds to the hex code #CC7A8B.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 48% green and 55% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 347° (out of 360°), 40% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 40% magenta, 32% yellow and 20% black pigments.
