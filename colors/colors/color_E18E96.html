Ruddy Pink

#E18E96

Ruddy Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Ruddy Pink corresponds to the hex code #E18E96.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 88% red, 56% green and 59% blue components.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 37% magenta, 33% yellow and 12% black pigments.
