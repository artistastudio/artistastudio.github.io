Fuchsia

#ED0DD9

Fuchsia belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Fuchsia corresponds to the hex code #ED0DD9.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 93% red, 5% green and 85% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 305° (out of 360°), 95% saturation and 93% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 95% magenta, 8% yellow and 7% black pigments.
