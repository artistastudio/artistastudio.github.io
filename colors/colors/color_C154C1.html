Deep Fuchsia

#C154C1

Deep Fuchsia belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Deep Fuchsia corresponds to the hex code #C154C1.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 33% green and 76% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 299° (out of 360°), 56% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 56% magenta, 0% yellow and 24% black pigments.
