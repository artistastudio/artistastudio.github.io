Dark Forest Green

#254117

Dark Forest Green belongs to the color family Dark Spring Green. It is of a low brightness and a medium saturation.
Dark Forest Green corresponds to the hex code #254117.

In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 100° (out of 360°), 65% saturation and 25% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 43% cyan, 0% magenta, 65% yellow and 75% black pigments.
