Sandy Yellow

#FDEE73

Sandy Yellow belongs to the color family Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Sandy Yellow corresponds to the hex code #FDEE73.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 6% magenta, 55% yellow and 1% black pigments.
