Purplish Pink

#CE5DAE

Purplish Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Purplish Pink corresponds to the hex code #CE5DAE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 81% red, 36% green and 68% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 316° (out of 360°), 55% saturation and 81% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 55% magenta, 16% yellow and 19% black pigments.
