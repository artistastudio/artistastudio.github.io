Persian Red

#CC3333

Persian Red belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Persian Red corresponds to the hex code #CC3333.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 20% green and 20% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 0° (out of 360°), 75% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 75% magenta, 75% yellow and 20% black pigments.
