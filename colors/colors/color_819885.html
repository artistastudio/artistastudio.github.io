Spanish Green

#819885

Spanish Green belongs to the color family Pastel Green. It is of a medium brightness and a low saturation.
Spanish Green corresponds to the hex code #819885.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 51% red, 60% green and 52% blue components.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 15% cyan, 0% magenta, 12% yellow and 40% black pigments.
