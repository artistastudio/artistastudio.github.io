Foggy Grey

#CBCAB6

Foggy Grey belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Foggy Grey corresponds to the hex code #CBCAB6.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 79% green and 71% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 57° (out of 360°), 10% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 0% magenta, 10% yellow and 20% black pigments.
