Hot Magenta

#F504C9

Hot Magenta belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Hot Magenta corresponds to the hex code #F504C9.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 96% red, 2% green and 79% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 310° (out of 360°), 98% saturation and 96% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 98% magenta, 18% yellow and 4% black pigments.
