Bright Grey

#3C4151

Bright Grey belongs to the color family Dark Pastel Sky Blue. It is of a low brightness and a low saturation.
Bright Grey corresponds to the hex code #3C4151.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 24% red, 25% green and 32% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 225° (out of 360°), 26% saturation and 32% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 26% cyan, 20% magenta, 0% yellow and 68% black pigments.
