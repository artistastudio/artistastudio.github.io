Light Tan

#FBEEAC

Light Tan belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Light Tan corresponds to the hex code #FBEEAC.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 98% red, 93% green and 67% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 50° (out of 360°), 31% saturation and 98% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 5% magenta, 31% yellow and 2% black pigments.
