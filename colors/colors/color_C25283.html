Bashful Pink

#C25283

Bashful Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Bashful Pink corresponds to the hex code #C25283.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 76% red, 32% green and 51% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 333° (out of 360°), 58% saturation and 76% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 58% magenta, 32% yellow and 24% black pigments.
