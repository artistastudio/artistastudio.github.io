Cornell Red

#B31B1B

Cornell Red belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Cornell Red corresponds to the hex code #B31B1B.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 85% magenta, 85% yellow and 30% black pigments.
