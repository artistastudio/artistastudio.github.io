Tangerine

#E78A61

Tangerine belongs to the color family Orange. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Tangerine corresponds to the hex code #E78A61.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 91% red, 54% green and 38% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 18° (out of 360°), 58% saturation and 91% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 40% magenta, 58% yellow and 9% black pigments.
