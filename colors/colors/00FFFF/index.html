Artista Cyan

#00FFFF

Artista Cyan belongs to the color family Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Artista Cyan corresponds to the hex code #00FFFF.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 0% red, 100% green and 100% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 180° (out of 360°), 100% saturation and 100% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 100% cyan, 0% magenta, 0% yellow and 0% black pigments.
