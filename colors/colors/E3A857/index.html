Indian Yellow

#E3A857

Indian Yellow belongs to the color family Orange. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Indian Yellow corresponds to the hex code #E3A857.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 89% red, 66% green and 34% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 35° (out of 360°), 62% saturation and 89% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 26% magenta, 62% yellow and 11% black pigments.
