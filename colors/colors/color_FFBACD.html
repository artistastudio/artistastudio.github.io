Pastel Pink

#FFBACD

Pastel Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Pastel Pink corresponds to the hex code #FFBACD.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 100% red, 73% green and 80% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 343° (out of 360°), 27% saturation and 100% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 27% magenta, 20% yellow and 0% black pigments.
