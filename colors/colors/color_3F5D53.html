Mineral Green

#3F5D53

Mineral Green belongs to the color family Dark Pastel Ocean Green. It is of a medium brightness and a low saturation.
Mineral Green corresponds to the hex code #3F5D53.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 32% cyan, 0% magenta, 11% yellow and 64% black pigments.
