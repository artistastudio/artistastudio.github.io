Pink Purple

#DB4BDA

Pink Purple belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Pink Purple corresponds to the hex code #DB4BDA.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 86% red, 29% green and 85% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 300° (out of 360°), 66% saturation and 86% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 66% magenta, 0% yellow and 14% black pigments.
