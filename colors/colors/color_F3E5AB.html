Vanilla

#F3E5AB

Vanilla belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Vanilla corresponds to the hex code #F3E5AB.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 95% red, 90% green and 67% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 48° (out of 360°), 30% saturation and 95% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 6% magenta, 30% yellow and 5% black pigments.
