Dusty Pink

#D58A94

Dusty Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Dusty Pink corresponds to the hex code #D58A94.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 35% magenta, 31% yellow and 16% black pigments.
