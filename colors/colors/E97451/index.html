Burnt Sienna

#E97451

Burnt Sienna belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Burnt Sienna corresponds to the hex code #E97451.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 91% red, 45% green and 32% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 14° (out of 360°), 65% saturation and 91% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 50% magenta, 65% yellow and 9% black pigments.
