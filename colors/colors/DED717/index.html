Barberry

#DED717

Barberry belongs to the color family Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Barberry corresponds to the hex code #DED717.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 3% magenta, 90% yellow and 13% black pigments.
