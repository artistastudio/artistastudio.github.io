Dark Jungle Green

#1A2421

Dark Jungle Green belongs to the color family Dark Pastel Ocean Green. It is of a low brightness and a low saturation.
Dark Jungle Green corresponds to the hex code #1A2421.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 10% red, 14% green and 13% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 162° (out of 360°), 28% saturation and 14% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 28% cyan, 0% magenta, 8% yellow and 86% black pigments.
