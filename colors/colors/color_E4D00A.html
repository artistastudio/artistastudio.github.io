Citrine

#E4D00A

Citrine belongs to the color family Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Citrine corresponds to the hex code #E4D00A.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 89% red, 82% green and 4% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 54° (out of 360°), 96% saturation and 89% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 9% magenta, 96% yellow and 11% black pigments.
