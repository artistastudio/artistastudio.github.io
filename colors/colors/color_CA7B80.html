Dirty Pink

#CA7B80

Dirty Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Dirty Pink corresponds to the hex code #CA7B80.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 79% red, 48% green and 50% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 355° (out of 360°), 39% saturation and 79% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 39% magenta, 37% yellow and 21% black pigments.
