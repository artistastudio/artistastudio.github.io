Soft Pink

#FDB0C0

Soft Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Soft Pink corresponds to the hex code #FDB0C0.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 99% red, 69% green and 75% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 347° (out of 360°), 30% saturation and 99% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 30% magenta, 24% yellow and 1% black pigments.
