Royal Blue

#2B60DE

Royal Blue belongs to the color family Sky Blue. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Royal Blue corresponds to the hex code #2B60DE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 17% red, 38% green and 87% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 222° (out of 360°), 81% saturation and 87% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 81% cyan, 57% magenta, 0% yellow and 13% black pigments.
