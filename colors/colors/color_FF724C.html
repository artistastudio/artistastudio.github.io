Pinkish Orange

#FF724C

Pinkish Orange belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Pinkish Orange corresponds to the hex code #FF724C.

In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 13° (out of 360°), 70% saturation and 100% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 55% magenta, 70% yellow and 0% black pigments.
