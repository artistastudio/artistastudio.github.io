Aquamarine Blue

#71D9E2

Aquamarine Blue belongs to the color family Pastel Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Aquamarine Blue corresponds to the hex code #71D9E2.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 44% red, 85% green and 89% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 184° (out of 360°), 50% saturation and 89% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 50% cyan, 4% magenta, 0% yellow and 11% black pigments.
