Satin Linen

#E6E4D4

Satin Linen belongs to the color family Pastel Yellow. It is of a high brightness and a low saturation.
Satin Linen corresponds to the hex code #E6E4D4.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 90% red, 89% green and 83% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 53° (out of 360°), 8% saturation and 90% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 1% magenta, 8% yellow and 10% black pigments.
