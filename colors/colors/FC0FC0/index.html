Shocking Pink

#FC0FC0

Shocking Pink belongs to the color family Magenta. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Shocking Pink corresponds to the hex code #FC0FC0.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 99% red, 6% green and 75% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 314° (out of 360°), 94% saturation and 99% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 94% magenta, 24% yellow and 1% black pigments.
