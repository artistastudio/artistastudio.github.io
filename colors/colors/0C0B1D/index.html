Ebony

#0C0B1D

Ebony belongs to the color family Dark Blue. It is of a low brightness and a medium saturation.
Ebony corresponds to the hex code #0C0B1D.

In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 59% cyan, 62% magenta, 0% yellow and 89% black pigments.
