Dusty Rose

#C0737A

Dusty Rose belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Dusty Rose corresponds to the hex code #C0737A.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 75% red, 45% green and 48% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 354° (out of 360°), 40% saturation and 75% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 40% magenta, 36% yellow and 25% black pigments.
