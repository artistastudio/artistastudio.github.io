Indian Red

#CD5C5C

Indian Red belongs to the color family Red. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Indian Red corresponds to the hex code #CD5C5C.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 80% red, 36% green and 36% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 0° (out of 360°), 55% saturation and 80% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 55% magenta, 55% yellow and 20% black pigments.
