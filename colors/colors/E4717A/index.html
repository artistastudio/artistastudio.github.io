Candy Pink

#E4717A

Candy Pink belongs to the color family Pastel Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Candy Pink corresponds to the hex code #E4717A.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 89% red, 44% green and 48% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 354° (out of 360°), 50% saturation and 89% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 50% magenta, 46% yellow and 11% black pigments.
