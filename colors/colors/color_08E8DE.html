Bright Turquoise

#08E8DE

Bright Turquoise belongs to the color family Cyan. It is of a high brightness and a high saturation.
Bright Turquoise corresponds to the hex code #08E8DE.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 3% red, 91% green and 87% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 177° (out of 360°), 97% saturation and 91% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 97% cyan, 0% magenta, 4% yellow and 9% black pigments.
