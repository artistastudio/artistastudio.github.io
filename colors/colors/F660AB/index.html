Hot Pink

#F660AB

Hot Pink belongs to the color family Crimson. It is of a high brightness and a medium saturation.
Hot Pink corresponds to the hex code #F660AB.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 96% red, 38% green and 67% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 329° (out of 360°), 61% saturation and 96% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 0% cyan, 61% magenta, 30% yellow and 4% black pigments.
