Egyptian Blue

#1034A6

Egyptian Blue belongs to the color family Sky Blue. It is of a medium brightness and a high saturation.
Egyptian Blue corresponds to the hex code #1034A6.
In the additive (digital) color space RGB, it is composed of 6% red, 20% green and 65% blue components.
In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 225° (out of 360°), 90% saturation and 65% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 90% cyan, 69% magenta, 0% yellow and 35% black pigments.
