Grass Green

#3F9B0B

Grass Green belongs to the color family Spring Green. It is of a medium brightness and a high saturation.
Grass Green corresponds to the hex code #3F9B0B.

In the alternative HSV representation, it is associated with a hue angle of 98° (out of 360°), 93% saturation and 61% value.
In the subtractive (process) color space CMYK, it is composed of 59% cyan, 0% magenta, 93% yellow and 39% black pigments.
