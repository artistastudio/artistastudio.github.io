Authentic designs

Artista Studio is a one smart artist. She relies on extensive literature research when making recommendations for your brand and target audience, and her designs are authentic as she is not using templates. She generates content on the go, and the longer you interact with her, the better she gets to know your brand and your preferences.

Designs in a matter of clicks

Artista Studio resides on a cloud (computing platform) and she is able to create designs in a matter of seconds. The users get to pick their preferred designs - within the constraints of their brand and target audience - which she then uses to re-generate new designs. Our users don't drag-and-drop, they just click and re-iterate.

Designs coming to life

Artista Studio is fascinated by moving objects and she understands that if you would like your content to stand out, creating a story is the best way to achieve that. After generating a static design, she is able to animate the same design and play it back to you as it comes to life.
